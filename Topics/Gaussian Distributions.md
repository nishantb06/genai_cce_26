## Multivariate Gaussian distribution
$$  
\mathbf{x} \sim \mathcal{N}(\boldsymbol{\mu}, \boldsymbol{\Sigma})  
$$

$$
p(\mathbf{x})
=
\frac{1}
{(2\pi)^{d/2} |\boldsymbol{\Sigma}|^{1/2}}
\exp\!\left(
-\frac{1}{2}
(\mathbf{x}-\boldsymbol{\mu})^\top
\boldsymbol{\Sigma}^{-1}
(\mathbf{x}-\boldsymbol{\mu})
\right)
$$
