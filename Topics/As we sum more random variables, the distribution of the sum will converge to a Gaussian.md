Imagine we plot the histogram of throwing just one dice, of course it will be a flat line since it is a uniform distribution. But as we throw more and more dice and plot the histogram of the sum of the numbers that appear on the face of die then the histogram will be closer and closer to that of a bell curve.

If we have $N$ random variables, then the sum $Z = X_1 + X_2 + \cdots + X_N$ will have a PDF that is the result of $N$ convolutions of all the individual PDFs.
