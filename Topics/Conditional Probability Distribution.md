For two [[Random Variables]] $X$ and $Y$, the conditional distribution of $Y$ given $X = a$ is:

$$P_{Y|X}(b \mid a) = \frac{P_{XY}(a, b)}{P_X(a)} \quad \text{when } P_X(a) > 0 \quad \text{(conditional)}$$
$$p_\theta(x, z) = p_\theta(x)\, p(z) \implies p(z|x) = \frac{p(x,z)}{p(x)}$$
This is also the [[Bayes Rule]]
