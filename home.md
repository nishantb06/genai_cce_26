## Overview 

### !!Develop a mathematical foundation for Generative Modelling!!

This course provides an in-depth exploration of deep generative models, including their probabilistic foundations and learning algorithms. Students will learn about various types of deep generative models such as variational autoencoders, generative adversarial networks, autoregressive models, diffusion models, and large language models with RLHF.

The course covers both **mathematical foundations** and **practical implementations** using popular frameworks like PyTorch. Students gain hands-on experience through lectures and assignments, exploring deep generative models across various AI tasks.
